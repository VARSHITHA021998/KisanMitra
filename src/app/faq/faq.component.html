FAQ

  1. How KisanMithra will operate?

    KisanMithra is an electronic trading platform created for connecting Farmers, Retailers digitally. It will offer plug-ins to any market yard existing in the state. Necessary customization shall be undertaken to conform to the regulations of each of the state.

  2. What are key objectives of this scheme?

    To catalyze private investment in setting up of agri-business project. Increasing rural income and employment. To strengthen backward linkage of agri-business project with producers. Assign producer groups and agriculture graduates to enhance their participation in value chain.

**FAQs for Bidders

  1. How to participate in the auction?

    The basic step is to register yourself in the website.

  2. What is the minimum bidding amount?

    The minimum amount for bidding will be Rs.10,000 (face value) and in multiples in Rs.10,000.

  3. How many bids can an investor make under this scheme?

    An investor can make only a single bid at a time in each specified auction.
